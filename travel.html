Argentina

The Argentine flag is a horizontal bicolour with in the center an emblem. The used colors in the flag are blue, white. The proportion of the Argentine flag is 9:14. The Flag of Argentina was adopted in 1818. The first use of the current flag design was in 1812. The last change to the current Argentine flag design was in 2012.
